Genre: Top Down Scrolling Shooter


Name: For Freedom


Goal: Blast and navigate your spaceship past obstacles and enemy ships to the alien forward base. Once there, battle their hordes of defenses to defeat them. 


Mechanics: Move the ship on the screen with the four cardinal directions, and fire lasers from your ship to clear whatever is in front of you. 


Levels: 
1) Moving towards alien homeworld - classic scrolling, debris/mines and spaceships appear from above

2) At homeworld - static location, must avoid attacks from all sides while destroying the core of their spaceship.
